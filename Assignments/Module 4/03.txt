Question=  What is the difference between OOP and POP?
ans:- 
OOP: 1> OOP stands for Object Oriented Programing.
     2> OOP follows bottom up approach.	
     3> A program is divided to objects and their interactions.	
     4> Inheritance is supported.
     5> Access control is supported via access modifiers.	
     6> Encapsulation is used to hide data.	
     7> C++, Java...	


pop: 1> POP stands for Procedural Oriented Programming.
     2> POP follows top down approach.
     3> A program is divided into funtions and they interacts.
     4> Inheritance is not supported.
     5> No access modifiers are supported.
     6> No data hiding present. Data is globally accessible.
     7> C, Pascal...
